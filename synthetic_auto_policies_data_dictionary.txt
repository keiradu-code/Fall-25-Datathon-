id = policy key

Numerical__________________________________
-   veh_value = market value of vehicle (in $10,000s)
-   exposure = basic unit of risk underyling an insurance premium
-   max_power = max horsepower of vehicles
-   driving_history_score = driving score based on past driving history (higher is better)
-   credit_score = credit score
-   numclaims = number of claims per policy key
-   claimcst0 = claim amount per policy key


Categorical__________________________
-   veh_body = type of vehicles
-   veh_age = age of vehicles (1 = youngest, 4 = oldest)
-   gender = gender of driver
-   area = driving area of residence
-   agecat = driver's age category from young (1) to old (6)
-   engine_type = engine type of vehicles
-   veh_color = color of vehicle
-   marital_status = status of driver (M = married, S = single)
-   time_of_week_driven = most frequent driving data of the week (weekdays vs weekend)
-   time_driven = most frequent driving time of the day
-   trm_len = term length (6-month vs 12-month policies)
-   low_education_ind = indicator of low vs high education



0/1 Binary_______________________________________
-   e_bill = indicator of paperless billing (0 = no, 1 = yes)
-   clm = indicator of claim (0 = no, 1 = yes)
